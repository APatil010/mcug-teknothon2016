ABOUT US

MIT Computer Users Group (MCUG) is a Student Body executing in the Computer Department of our college. Working towards achieving the ultimate goal of having a fully functional and academically self sustainable Departmental Body, our motto boasts our priorities, "Sharing Knowledge, Bridging Gaps".

WHAT WE DO:

In accordance with our motto, we bring you two of the most awaited events in University Academia, Teknothon & Linuxication.
Our thirst for knowledge is never ending, and we urge you to feel the same. Approach with your doubts, and we promise to clarify. We also organize various technological sessions to aid the students in keeping themselves updated.
